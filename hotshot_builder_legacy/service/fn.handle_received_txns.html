Expand description

Utility function that will take the given list of transactions, txns, wraps them in a ReceivedTransaction struct and attempt to broadcast them to the given transaction [BroadcastSender] tx_sender. The broadcast itself it a non-blocking operation, and any failures of the broadcast are collected into the returned vector of Results.

There is also a max_txn_len parameter that is used to check to ensure that transactions that exceed this threshold will also not be broadcasted.
